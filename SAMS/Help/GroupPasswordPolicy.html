Password requirements

Magnon Account passwords must be at least 8 characters long. Password must include:

  • Minimum 1 Upper Case Letters, which includes A - Z
  • Minimum 1 Numeric Character, which includes 0 - 9
  • Minimum 1 Special Character, which includes ! " # $ % & ‘ ( ) * + , - . / : ; < = > ? @ [ \ ] ^ { | } ~

Other features or conditions are:

  • Login Expires after every 60 days
  • Show Expiry Warning message before 10 days of Expiry
  • User won't be able to reuse a password from the last 3 passwords
  • Unused user account will be disabled after 90 days
  • Users are provided with 3 Unsuccessful attempts to login before account gets locked

Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

Question: How often should I change my password?

Answer: As per Group Password Policy, user password expires in every 60 days, so it is recommended to change before that.

Question: What happens if I don't change my password in 60 days span?

Answer: Your account will be automatically locked if password is not changed in 60 days.

Question: What should I do if my account is locked or if receive message 'Your account has been disabled Please contact Admin'?

Answer: You need to contact Magnon Administrator to get your account unlocked.

Question: Can I reuse my previous password?

Answer: As per password policy system doesn't allow to re-user your 3 previous most recent passwords.

Question: What should I do if I forget my password?

Answer: You need to contact Magnon Administrator to get your password reset.

Question: What will happen if I apply a wrong password while login?

Answer: You are permitted with 3 Unsuccessful attempts to login, after which your account get locked.

Question: What happens to my Magnon Account if it is not in use for some time?

Answer: Unused accounts in Magnon are automatically disabled after 90 days; in this case, you need to contact Magnon Administrator to get your account re-activated.
